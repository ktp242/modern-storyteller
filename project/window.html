Window
"Relationships are like windows, sometimes they open, sometimes they close, and sometimes they even break into pieces." “Window” is a hand-drawn 2.5 D narrative animation video that explores this theme and reminds people that when looking through windows they not only see what’s outside them but also their own reflections. Perhaps this experience can help people think more deeply about relationships and how important it is to take care of them.

Jelly Wei 衛潔莉


Jelly Wei is a visual artist and motion graphics designer born and educated in Taiwan, based in New York City. She received her MFA in Computer Art with a concentration in Motion Graphics from The School of Visual Arts in New York in 2016. Wei’s art works seamlessly blend the sense of graphic and vivid color with her engaging visual language.

Exhibition Date: July 30-31, 2016
