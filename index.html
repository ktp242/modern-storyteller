Modern Storyteller is an exhibition created to give designers the opportunity to showcase types of new media and interactive communication within points of interest of their project that will inspire, motivate and educate the next generation.

The exhibition is organized by a team of young professionals armed with various expertise and personal pursuits. Carrying over the essence and ambition from three exhibitions that we have previously hosted—±12 (2014), Zero Boundaries (2014) and Level Up (2015)—this exhibition aims to support and stimulate the creative energy amongst aspiring young designers from Taiwan in the United States.

We cordially welcome you to submit your work and seize the opportunity to show off your talent! Please follow the Call for Entry tab and find out how to submit online
